Affordable Housing Advisory

We offer clients specialized and scalable operational solutions that help our clients secure capital, optimize project viability, and access specialized programs across Canada including CMHC’s affordable housing initiatives.

We work closely with clients to understand their business model, project economics, and long-term goals, transforming them into strong, lender-ready and grant-ready submissions.

  • Business Loan Advisory
  • Grant Writing & Funding Applications for affordable housing
  • Affordable Housing Advisory (CMHC)
  • Project Viability & Capital Strategy
