Chris A. Soboh

COO / Managing Partner

Email Chris LinkedIn Profile

Albert Chris Soboh is a corporate lawyer and business executive with a multidisciplinary background in law, strategy, and brand development. At Akili Group, he brings a structured and creative approach to business growth, combining legal expertise with strategic insight to support clients, partners, and portfolio companies in achieving long-term value creation.

Chris’ experience spans corporate advisory, partnerships, and institutional development. Beyond the boardroom, he serves as Editor-in-Chief of EMY Africa Magazine and plays a leading role at EMY Africa.

He is also a Director at Carbon AV Limited and Co-Founder of Each One, Reach One, an initiative focused on mentorship and financial literacy for young people.
